CardForge

Batch-generate print-ready business cards from Excel spreadsheets. Free, open-source, runs entirely on your machine.

從 Excel 批次產生印刷級名片。免費開源，完全本機運行。

View on GitHub Quick Start
📊

Excel Import

Upload your .xlsx file. Columns are auto-mapped to card fields — no manual config needed.

🎨

Live Visual Editor

Drag fields, adjust fonts, colors, and alignment in real time. WYSIWYG card design.

🖨️

Batch Export

Generate PDF + JPEG for every employee at once. 300 DPI, print-ready quality.

🖼️

Custom Templates

Upload your own front and back card backgrounds. Full control over the design.

🔒

100% Local

Your data never leaves your machine. No cloud, no accounts, no subscriptions.

💻

Cross-Platform

Works on macOS, Windows, and Linux with a single startup command.
